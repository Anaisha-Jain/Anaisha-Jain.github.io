Kinetik - Your very own supplement Tracker!

Flutter Dart Firebase Firestore Cloud Functions Git Python Pharmacokinetics Javascript Mobile UI/UX Design Data Modelling

Abstract

I noticed that standard health and habit trackers are completely static, and they simply tell you what you consumed, but not how it actually behaves in your body. To solve this, I built Kinetik, a Flutter and Firebase app that tracks real-time supplement concentration instead of just doses. The biggest technical hurdle was taking abstract pharmacokinetic formulas and translating them into a dynamic, real-time visual graph on a mobile device while keeping the database reads and writes highly optimized. It really challenged me to bridge complex data modeling with clean mobile development, and the end product was an app that took in your age, weight, metabolism and graphed out curved that helped you visualised when the supplements was best being absorbed and utilised in your body. Furthermore, the app told users when to cap their consumption, and even gave them a focus tab to maximise their work based on what career path they were in!

Methodology

  • Engineered a real-time pharmacokinetic (PK) tracking application using Flutter and Dart to compute and visualize metabolic decay and blood concentration curves for user-inputted supplements.
  • Architected a scalable NoSQL database schema in Firebase Firestore to manage user profiles, multi-substance intake logs, and granular, real-time concentration data points.
  • Implemented localized mathematical modeling equations to simulate half-life, absorption rates, and receptor clearance, transforming static time-stamps into dynamic line charts.
  • Designed clean, responsive user interfaces focused on data visualization, ensuring seamless rendering of complex, multi-variable metabolic graphs across iOS and Android devices.
