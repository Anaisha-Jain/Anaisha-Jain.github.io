About
Me

I am a nanotechnology engineering student at the University of Waterloo building at the intersection of hardware, code, and biology. I spend my time designing DNA aptamer systems, engineering robotic integrations, and developing bioinformatics pipelines, because I truly believe the most important problems in the world are solved at the scale of 10⁻⁹ metres. When I step away from the lab bench or my IDE, I'm usually building projects, hanging out with my friends at the library, or falling down a deep Wikipedia rabbit hole about Dinosaurs!
