About
Me

I am a nanobiotechnology engineer fascinated by the interface of biology and engineering at the molecular scale. My work sits at the intersection of biosensors and bioinformatics, where I design DNA aptamer systems and robotic integrations. I believe the most important problems in the world are solved at the scale of 10⁻⁹ metres.
